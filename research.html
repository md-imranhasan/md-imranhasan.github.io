Research Projects

Internship Project: MicroRNAs in COVID-19

This project explored **microRNA (miRNA) involvement in COVID-19 and respiratory diseases**. Used **linear regression models in R** to identify miRNAs linked to **immune modulation and disease severity**. Applied **machine learning (Random Forest, SVM, Gradient Boosting)** to predict vaccine response based on miRNA-mRNA interactions.

HIV Project: Single-Cell RNA Sequencing & Biomarker Discovery

Conducted **scRNA-seq analysis** to study **gene expression in immune cells of HIV-infected patients**. Applied **Gene Ontology (GO) enrichment and KEGG pathway analysis** to identify **biological processes & pathways**. Built **Protein-Protein Interaction (PPI) networks in Cytoscape** and identified potential drug targets via **molecular docking**.

Thesis Project: Computational Analysis of Staphylococcus aureus Resistance

Studied **antibiotic-resistant strains of Staphylococcus aureus** using **genome analysis & network biology**. Identified **antimicrobial resistance (AMR) genes** and **hub genes (mgrA, arlR, arlS, sarA)** for drug targeting. Performed **molecular docking** to assess drug interactions with AMR-related proteins.
